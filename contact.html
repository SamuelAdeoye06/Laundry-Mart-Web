Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries.

How soon can I schedule a service?

You can schedule a service at anytime during our operating hours. Our operating hours are 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and services can be rendered as soon as 24 hours after booking, depending on availability. We recommend booking at least a day in advance to secure your preferred date and time.

What areas do you currently serve?

We currently provide cleaning services within Lagos State only. If you're located anywhere in Lagos, feel free to contact us to confirm availability in your specific area.

How do payments and pricing work?

Pricing depends on the type of cleaning service requested (rooms, houses, cars, furniture, etc.) and the scope of the job. Once your booking is confirmed, payment details will be provided. Payment is made after a successful booking confirmation through our website or any of our official communication channels
